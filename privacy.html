Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

MeetScheduling (“MeetScheduling”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the MeetScheduling platform available at https://www.meetscheduling.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and scheduling platform.

By using MeetScheduling, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our scheduling services.

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile information
  • Account preferences

This information is used to create and manage your MeetScheduling account.

Calendar Integration Data

MeetScheduling allows users to connect third-party calendar services such as Google Calendar.

When you connect your calendar, we may access limited calendar data including:

  • Calendar availability
  • Event start and end times
  • Permission to create calendar events

This access allows MeetScheduling to:

  • Schedule meetings
  • Create calendar events
  • Prevent double bookings
  • Synchronize availability with your calendar

MeetScheduling does not access or store private event descriptions, attendee lists, or event content beyond what is necessary to provide scheduling functionality.

Usage Data

We may automatically collect certain technical information including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited
  • Usage activity on the platform

This information helps us improve the performance, security, and reliability of our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide scheduling services
  • Allow users to create booking links
  • Allow invitees to schedule meetings
  • Create calendar events automatically
  • Send meeting confirmations and reminders
  • Improve the performance and reliability of the platform
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent abuse or fraudulent activity

MeetScheduling does not sell personal data.

3. Google User Data

MeetScheduling integrates with Google APIs, including Google Calendar.

When you connect your Google account, MeetScheduling may request permission to access your calendar through Google OAuth.

This permission allows MeetScheduling to:

  • Create calendar events for scheduled meetings
  • Update or cancel events when bookings change
  • Prevent double booking by checking calendar availability

MeetScheduling only uses Google user data to provide scheduling functionality.

MeetScheduling does not use Google data for advertising, marketing, or profiling purposes.

MeetScheduling’s use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

We may share limited information with trusted service providers that help us operate our platform, including:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Email delivery providers
  • Analytics services

These providers are required to maintain confidentiality and protect your data.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure HTTPS encrypted connections
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Restricted access to sensitive data

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

7. Third-Party Services

MeetScheduling may integrate with third-party services such as:

  • Google Calendar
  • Video meeting platforms
  • Email service providers

These services operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
